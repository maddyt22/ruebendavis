Brief

The design should align with the unconventional and experimental style of David Carson, a renowned American graphic designer known for his innovative work in the 1990s, particularly as the art director for "Ray Gun" magazine.

Solution

For the title page, I used my own imagery to create a collage featuring a wave and a portrait of Carson, symbolising his passion for surfing.
