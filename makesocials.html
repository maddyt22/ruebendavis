Make Socials

Logo Design

Brief

Make Socials, a thriving social media management business, sought a rebranding effort transitioning from Make Marketing to Make Socials. The task involved creating a fresh logo design reflecting Marisa's spirited personality and the professionalism of her business.

Solution

Create a logo blending funky and professional aesthetics, characterised by dynamic curved lines and a bold logo mark merging the letters 'M' and 'S.' The chosen typeface enhances the funky feel, ensuring a cohesive visual identity. Drawing inspiration from Marisa's existing brand hues, the colour palette injects vibrancy while maintaining brand continuity.
