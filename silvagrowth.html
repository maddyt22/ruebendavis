Silva Growth

Logo Design

Brief

Silva Growth required a redesigned logo to effectively represent his brand, encompassing the core elements of strength, conditioning, and jiu-jitsu training. The client sought a design that prominently featured the letters S and G, along with a subtle incorporation of a jiu-jitsu belt that could change colour to signify his progression through the jiu-jitsu belts.

Solution

Crafted a sleek and modern logo that elegantly integrates both the S and G, while subtly showcasing a jiu-jitsu belt. This design not only captures the essence of strength and progression but also adapts dynamically to reflect the client's advancement in jiu-jitsu ranks.
