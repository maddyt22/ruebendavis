Maxum Watches

Stills Photographer

Maxum, celebrating 25 years in the watch industry, seeks to rebrand and emphasize its enduring commitment to design, quality, and craftsmanship. The project involves showcasing Maxum’s five pillars - Fashion, Sport, Art, Music, and Film - through behind-the-scenes stills for social media.

I captured candid moments between models, creating an authentic and relatable feel that highlights the usage of Maxum watches and the brand's ethos. Using both digital and film photography, I produced a collection of images that effectively convey the brand’s commitment to style and quality, resonating with its audience and enhancing its social media presence.
