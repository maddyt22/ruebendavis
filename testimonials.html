Lisa Bennett

Lisa Bennett Studio

Working with Rueben on my promotional video for my art exhibition, Rhythmic Landscapes was fun and seamless.

I gave him a rough brief of what I was thinking of. He suggested locations where he thought would best suit my story, he filmed in different formats, edited graphics and added my voice over. All with an amazing creative flair but still in an professional way. I was over the moon with the final product.

The video was used throughout my social media campaign and had great success with bringing many collectors to view my artworks.

Thank you Rueben for all your hard work that captured my story on film in a truly magical way.
