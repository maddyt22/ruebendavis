Stolen Goods

Web Layout

Brief

Tasked with creating an adaptive festival web design, influenced by the minimalist, electric, underground vibes of Berlin nightclubs. The brief encouraged taking risks with the branding to achieve a distinctive result. Inspired by an event hosted by my mates at Stolen Goods. I decided to adapt the project from a festival site to an event-specific site while maintaining the original brief.

Solution

I designed an event-specific website that embraced a risk-taking approach, setting it apart from conventional sites. Inspired by the event's stage design, which featured retro televisions, each page was designed as a different "station," with navigation buttons at the bottom, providing users with an immersive journey reminiscent of channel surfing.
