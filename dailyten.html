The Daily 10

Branding

Brief

Create a logo design that incorporates elements of a tree and branding collateral for The Daily 10, a revolutionary mobile and web application designed to make daily math practice a habit with personalised questions aligned to the Australian curriculum.

Solution

The logo design combines two powerful symbols: the brain and the tree. The brain represents the pursuit of knowledge and intellectual development, while the tree symbolises growth and continuous learning. Together, these elements form a visual representation that embodies The Daily 10's core mission of fostering knowledge and personal evolution.
