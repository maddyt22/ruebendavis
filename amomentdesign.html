A Moment

Poster Design

Brief

Create three poster designs for a short film "A Moment," directed by Josh Lawrence. Capturing the essence of the narrative's themes of connection and loss. The goal is to artfully showcase the intertwining emotions of the protagonists, Cillian and Amelia, while encapsulating an old-school aesthetic.

Solution

Each poster design portrays the delicate balance between connection and disconnection experienced by Cillian and Amelia throughout the film. Drawing inspiration from the narrative, I incorporated an old-school aesthetic with subtle grainy film texture to evoke nostalgia and depth. The use of colour and composition harmonises with the film's theme, creating visually captivating posters that invite audiences to delve into the heart of this enchanting tale.
